# Senior Product Manager

## Responsibilities
- Lead the product vision and strategy for our AI-powered analytics platform.
- Work closely with engineering, design, and marketing teams.
- Define and track key metrics (KPIs) to measure product success.
- Conduct user research and usability testing.

## Requirements
- 5+ years of experience in Product Management.
- Strong technical background; experience with AI/ML products is a huge plus.
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills.
- Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies.
- Data-driven decision making.
